Welcome to the BMR Calculator

The BMR Calculator stands for Basal Metabolic Rate aka BMR for short. The purpose of Basal Metabolic Rate is to calculate the amount of energy utilized by the body while at rest. Typically Basal Metabolic Rates are calculated during a period of fasting - a minimum of 12 hours without food consumption - and it occurs when the digestive system is at rest. There are severeal factors that we will discuss below which impact your BMR. This includes age, height, sex and weight.

Factors that Affect BMR

Gender

Typically men have higher basal metabolic rate than women. This is because men tend to have more muscle and a larger body size. With higher muscle and body mass. Those with more muscle and body mass have a higher Basal Metabolic Rate since they typically have larger organs and more fluid volume to maintain.

Age

During childhood children experience extreme rates of metabolism. This is because as they grow they increase in body size which further increases metabolism. During adulthood, however, the BMR begins to decrease as humans experience hormonal and neurological changes.

Height & Weight

Height and Weight are large factors of the Basal Metabolic Rate as well. A taller person would have a higher BMR than that of a short person. This is because a taller person would have a larger surface area; larger organs; etc which requires more energy to sustain the body. Weight also affects BMR as well. Objects with larger weights tend to have higher mass (assuming they are all on Earth where the acceleration due to gravity is held constant). This means that people that weigh heavier tend to have a higher BMR since they have increased lean mass.

How BMR is calculated

There are two formulas used to calculate BMR. The formulas are distinct by gender - meaning that there is one formula to calculate BMR for men and another for female.

The formula to calculate BMR for men is as follows: 66.47 + (13.75 * weight [kg]) + (5.003 * size [cm]) − (6.755 * age [years])

The formula to calculate BMR for women is as follows: 655.1 + (9.563 * weight [kg]) + (1.85 * size [cm]) − (4.676 * age [years])
